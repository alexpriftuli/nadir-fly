Honest scope

What we can do today, plainly.

We are a one-operator firm. Day-1 capability is Open Category A3 — sites without uninvolved people in the flight area. Specific Category and BVLOS scopes are available on declaration, with planning lead times stated up front.

  • Day-1
    Open Cat A3 · today
    Single operator, single aircraft. C1-class equipment. Ground-mount and rural utility PV, fenced sites.
  • On request
    Specific Cat · on declaration
    STS-01 or PDRA-S01 declaration, 2–3 week planning. Rooftop arrays adjacent to public space, larger MTOW.
  • On request
    BVLOS · partner-supported
    Large-fleet and corridor campaigns delivered with a partnered observer team. Quoted per site.
  • Not offered
    Night / over-people · scope-out
    Not offered. Will refer to a vetted operator rather than over-promise.
